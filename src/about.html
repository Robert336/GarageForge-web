Our Story

GarageForge started with a simple belief: great ideas shouldn't be held back by expensive prototyping or long shipping delays. Based in Schomberg, Ontario, we're your local 3D printing partner serving the Greater Toronto Area with affordable, fast-turnaround services.

We understand the frustration of waiting weeks for prototypes or paying premium prices for simple parts. That's why we've built our business around rapid response and transparent pricing, helping startups, repair shops, educators, and makers bring their ideas to life quickly and affordably.
