Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you complete my project?

Most projects are completed within 24-48 hours. Rush orders can often be done the same day. Complex projects may take longer, but we'll give you an accurate timeline upfront.

What file formats do you accept?

We primarily work with STL files for 3D printing. We also accept OBJ, PLY, and 3MF formats. For design consultation, we can work with images, sketches, or existing parts.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! We can help with design modifications, optimization for 3D printing, and even create designs from scratch based on your requirements. Design consultation is included with custom projects.

What are your payment options?

We accept cash, e-transfer, and all major credit cards. Payment is typically due upon completion, though we may request a deposit for large or custom projects.

Can you deliver to my location?

We offer pickup in Schomberg and can arrange delivery throughout the GTA. Delivery fees depend on location and order size. Many clients find pickup convenient and cost-effective.

What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

Your satisfaction is our priority. We'll work with you to make it right, whether that means adjustments, reprints, or finding an alternative solution. We stand behind our work.
