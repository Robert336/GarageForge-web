Remote-Controlled Fan Turntable

We built a simple rotating platform that lets someone aim their bedroom fan with just a remote control. Our client needed a way to redirect airflow without getting up due to accessibility reasons, so we designed a custom circular base, set up the motor system, and wired everything together. The clever part? We used airsoft BBs as smooth bearings to make it spin effortlessly. It's a small device that makes a big difference in daily comfort.
